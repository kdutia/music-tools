music-tools

browser

  • spectrum-analyzer — upload a WAV file, get a spectrogram and prominent frequencies; useful for designing scales around recorded sounds
  • rational-intonation — explore just intonation systems and rational interval ratios in the browser

command line (python)

  • mg-convert — convert audio files to MakeNoise Morphagene format (48 kHz, 32-bit float, stereo WAV)
  • keyboard-rhythm-capture — capture keyboard input timing and export as MIDI or CSV
